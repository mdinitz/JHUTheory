  <h1>Welcome to the JHU Algorithms and Complexity Group</h1>
  <p>
    We are based in the <a href="https://www.cs.jhu.edu/">Department of Computer Science</a> and the 
    <a href="https://engineering.jhu.edu/ams/">Department of Applied Mathematics and Statistics</a> in the <a href="https://engineering.jhu.edu/">Whiting School of Engineering</a> at <a href="https://www.jhu.edu/">Johns Hopkins University</a>. Our faculty and students study a broad variety of problems in algorithms and complexity, with the aim of gaining insight into some of the most fundamental issues in computer science.
